Jaymal is a landscape and fine art photographer based between Geneva and London. The inspiration behind his photography comes from his travels around the natural world where he seeks to use photography as a form of meditation. His creation of images from these moments of inward reflection and inspiration goes beyond the surface, to discover the levels of meaning that lie beneath that which we see with our naked eye and to present the viewer with that same feeling of inner calm he himself seeks when in the field.
